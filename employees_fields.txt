Fields for Extended Employees Table
EmployeeID: Unique identifier for each employee
FirstName: First name of the employee
LastName: Last name of the employee
HireDate: Date the employee was hired
Department: Department of the employee (e.g., Sales, Engineering, HR)
Office_Location: Office location of the employee
Position: Position of the employee (e.g., Manager, Developer)
Salary: Salary of the employee
ReportsTo: EmployeeID of the person to whom this employee reports
Performance_Rating: Performance rating of the employee (out of 5)
Email: Email address of the employee
PhoneNumber: Phone number of the employee
Revenue_Generated: Revenue generated by the employee for the company
