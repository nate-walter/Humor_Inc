Fields for Extended Companies Table
CompanyID: Unique identifier for each company
Name: Name of the company
Description: Brief description of the company
Industry: Industry the company belongs to
FoundedDate: Date the company was founded
Headquarters: Where the company is headquartered
CEO_Name: Name of the CEO
Revenue_Mil: Revenue in millions
Stock_Symbol: Stock market symbol
Employee_Count: Number of employees
Market_Cap_Bil: Market capitalization in billions
HQ_Country: Country where the headquarters is located
