Fields for Extended Promotions Table
PromotionID: Unique identifier for each promotion
CompanyID: Foreign key linking to the Companies table
ServiceID: Foreign key linking to the Services table
StartDate: Start date of the promotion
EndDate: End date of the promotion
DiscountRate: Discount rate offered
PromotionType: Type of promotion (e.g., Seasonal, Clearance, Launch)
Active: Whether the promotion is currently active or not
