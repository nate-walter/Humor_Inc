Fields for Extended Clients Table
ClientID: Unique identifier for each client
FirstName: First name of the client
LastName: Last name of the client
BirthDate: Birthdate of the client
ZipCode: Zip code of the client's residence
PhoneNumber: Phone number of the client
Email: Email address of the client
Occupation: Occupation of the client
Client_Type: Type of client (Individual, Corporate)
Last_Purchase_Date: Last date the client made a purchase
Credit_Score: Credit score of the client
Total_Spent: Total amount spent by the client
