Fields for Extended Reviews Table
ReviewID: Unique identifier for each review
ClientID: Foreign key linking to the Clients table
ServiceID: Foreign key linking to the Services table
CompanyID: Foreign key linking to the Companies table
Rating: Rating given by the client (out of 5)
ReviewText: Text of the review
ReviewDate: Date the review was posted
HelpfulVotes: Number of helpful votes the review received
UnhelpfulVotes: Number of unhelpful votes the review received
