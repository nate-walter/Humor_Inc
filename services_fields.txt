Fields for Extended Services Table
ServiceID: Unique identifier for each service
CompanyID: Foreign key linking to the Companies table
ServiceName: Name of the service
Price: Price of the service
Subscription_Model: Type of subscription (One-time, Monthly, Yearly)
Rating: Customer rating out of 5
Discount: Discount percentage on the service
Availability_Area: Area where the service is available (Local, National, Global)
LaunchDate: Date the service was launched
Category: Category of the service (Software, Hardware, Food, etc.)
